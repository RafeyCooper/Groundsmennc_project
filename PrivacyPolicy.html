Privacy Policy - Groundsmen Landscaping NC

Effective Date: March 23, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

At Groundsmen Landscaping NC, we are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website or services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you provide (name, address, phone number, email)
  • Project details and service inquiries
  • Automatically collected data (IP address, browser type, device information)

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide services
  • Process transactions and schedule appointments
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send service-related updates and promotional communications

Text Message Communication

By providing your information or contacting us, you are agreeing to receive text messages from our business. Message frequency may vary. Standard message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt-out of text communications at any time.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. If you have security concerns, please contact us directly by phone.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers necessary to complete our landscaping services, who are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Review the personal information we have collected
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal obligations)

Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The updated version will be posted on our website with a revised effective date.
