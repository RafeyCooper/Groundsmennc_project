About Us

What Makes The Groundsmen Concord’s Top Landscapers?

The Groundsmen is a brother founded company that began in 2020 after the owners combined their previous experience in landscaping, agriculture, and marketing to bring a new level of professionalism to the local lawn care industry. We promise to deliver the best landscaping services you can find in the Concord area (and all of North Carolina).

  • Experienced landscaping team full of professionals.
  • Satisfaction guarantee: we promise you’ll love our landscaping work.

Our Mission

We aim to create a home service brand that excels in customer service and reliability. Through outreach, referrals, and good reviews, we will leave a mark of integrity, helping us to keep our prices competitive and have the largest impact we can on the community.

Why We Exist

There is an unacceptable lack of professionalism in the home service industry today. We’ve heard countless stories from friends and clients about the difficulties of working with handyman, contractors, and other service providers. This led us to embark on a mission of creating a brand that would be recognized for its customer service and authenticity.
